HandMonitor

Project Overview: The HandMonitor System

The HandMonitor is an innovative, low-cost haptic glove system designed to transform the landscape of stroke rehabilitation. By integrating advanced wearable technology with real-time data processing, the system provides immediate corrective feedback to enhance finger movement accuracy, functional grasping, and overall hand dexterity.

Redefining Stroke Recovery

Welcome to the future of accessible rehabilitation. HandMonitor is a research-driven, wearable system specifically engineered to help stroke survivors regain independence by restoring hand mobility.

Our Mission

Traditional rehabilitation can be costly and difficult to access. We aim to bridge the gap between hospital therapy and daily life through real-time technology that guides patients toward functional movement in a home-based environment.

Impact Statement

HandMonitor addresses the critical demand for a precise yet affordable rehabilitation tool. By moving sophisticated therapy from the clinic to the home, we provide a practical path toward motor autonomy.

Technical Architecture & Intelligent Design

Multimodal Fusion

Neoprene design integrating flex sensors, FSRs, and IMUs to capture comprehensive motion and force data.

High-Speed Haptics

Linear Resonant Actuators (LRAs) providing instantaneous localized vibrotactile feedback for real-time correction.

ML Communication

Low-latency BLE connection with Raspberry Pi powered by specialized machine learning algorithms.
